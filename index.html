Eric Dexheimer

I am a PhD student in the Dyson Robotics Lab at Imperial College London, supervised by Prof. Andrew J. Davison. I am interested in geometric computer vision, probabilistic inference, and nonlinear optimization. Previously, I completed my Masters in Robotics at Carnegie Mellon University under the supervision of Prof. Michael Kaess.
